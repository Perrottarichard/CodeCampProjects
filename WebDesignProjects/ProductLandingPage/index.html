Quality Assurance

At Fern's Online Bookstore, we strive to provide the highest standard of quality to all of our customers. To ensure this, we personally check each book by reading it before shipping to make sure it meets our standards. For this reason, some items may have highlighting, ear-marking, marginalia, or coffee stains.




How to Order

We make ordering your favorite books as easy as making a few clicks, entering your payment information (cash only), taking our survey, passing a background check, filling in our membership application (and getting approved), and then waiting for standard USPS mail to deliver the cash you've sent. Upon receipt of cash, our staff will then perform quality assurance. Once quality assurance is approved, we will ship your order on the 1st of the next month. If the item you ordered is not up to our standards, we will refund the money within two calendar years after receiving your signed and notarized application for a cash refund. Buying was never this easy!




Price Matching

Because we know you work hard for your money, and we strive to provide you with the best online shopping experience, we will match any of our competitors prices (excluding Amazon, eBay, Walmart, Barnes and Nobles, Lazada, Shopee, and most other major e-commerce retailers and wholesalers). All you have to do is submit the easy online form and we will respond within 23 working days after the next vernal equinox.
