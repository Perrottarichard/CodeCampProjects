Nilubon "Fern" Sukawanich

Guidance Counselor

“I am not what happened to me. I am what I choose to become.” -Carl Gustav Jung
  • Born 1983 in Bangkok, Thailand
  • At age 14, leaves Bangkok and moves north to Chiang Mai, Thailand
  • In 2001, begins undergraduate studies in the Faculty of Humanities at Chiang Mai University
  • In 2005 graduates with a BS in Psychology
  • Gives birth to a daughter, Namping, in September 2009
  • In 2013, begins work at Rajamangala University of Technology Lanna in Chiang Mai in the department of psychological counseling
  • In 2015, continues graduate education at Chiang Mai University
  • Receives an MS in Psychology from CMU in 2017
  • In 2018, in addition to her role as counselor at RMUTL, Fern begins volunteering as a counselor answering calls on the Samaritans Chiang Mai suicide-prevention hotline
